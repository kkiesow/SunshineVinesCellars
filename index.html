Sunshine Vines Cellars seeks to bring wine to the younger generation, with a variety of tasty flavors and portable packaging.

located in Sunny San Luis Obispo

Sunshine vines is a winery located in San Luis Obsipo County, hidden in the beautiful Arroyo Grande backroads. While we are smaller, we are growing rapidly. Our sleek, modern take on the wine scene has won us countless awards, including The Decanter Gold 2020's Regional Choice for our Sunshine Rosé. Our renowned, sustainable process has put us on the map early, given that we were established in 2019. Facing challenging conditions with the Covid-19 pandemic, we adjusted our business to give our customers a beneficial experience from their own kitchen. Some virtual events that we hosted included: wine and cheese pairings, Q & A with our wine-makers, and live tours of the grounds. We also held a virtual auction that made a record 120,000 towards wine club experience development. We grow, package, and sell our own wine, prioritizing the environment above all.
